Anomaly Detection

How can we improve the efficiency and reduce the cost of semiconductor manufacturing process while ensuring the quality of the final product?

Project Overview

In today's data-driven world, the ability to detect anomalies or outliers is crucial for businesses to maintain their operations and prevent costly errors. In this project, we will be working with a semiconductor manufacturing dataset, aiming to identify anomalous behavior that can lead to defects or failures in the manufacturing process. By leveraging machine learning algorithms and statistical analysis, we can develop a predictive model that alerts operators to potential issues before they occur, reducing downtime and improving overall production efficiency. Ultimately, this project aims to demonstrate the value of anomaly detection in the manufacturing industry and its potential to deliver significant business benefits.
