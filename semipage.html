Anomaly Detection

How can we improve the efficiency and reduce the cost of semiconductor manufacturing process while ensuring the quality of the final product?

Project Overview

This project is to improve the efficiency and productivity of semiconductor manufacturing processes by detecting and addressing potential anomalies. By identifying anomalies early, operators can take corrective action to prevent defects and reduce downtime, resulting in cost savings and increased customer satisfaction. Additionally, by implementing a predictive model, the manufacturing process can be optimized, allowing for greater output and reduced waste. This project aim is to identify patterns that may indicate potential failures or defects in the manufacturing process. To accomplish this task, I will explore the data, preprocess it, and select the most pertinent features, before constructing and evaluating multiple machine learning models to predict anomalies and classify them as either pass or fail. Ultimately, the use of machine learning and data analysis in anomaly detection can lead to significant business benefits, including improved quality control, increased efficiency, and higher profits.
