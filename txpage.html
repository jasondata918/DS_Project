Informative data visualization that allows users to monitor real-time traffic incidents in Austin, TX

Project Overview

This project is to create an interactive and informative data visualization that displays real-time traffic incident reports in Austin, Texas. Highlighting the most common types of incidents, their locations, and their statuses. This data set contains traffic incident information from the Austin-Travis County traffic reports RSS feed, available at http://www.ci.austin.tx.us/qact/default.cfm. The dataset is updated every 5 minutes. Incidents that are currently in the RSS feed have a status of "active" in this dataset. Incidents that are no longer appear in the feed have a status of "archived."

  • [DataSource] The Official City of Austin Open Data Portal - https://data.austintexas.gov
