Wine Quality Prediction

How can we leverage wine quality prediction to gain a competitive edge in the market?

Project Overview

Evaluating the quality of wine is a complex process that involves sensory analysis by expert tasters, which is time-consuming and expensive. The producer wants to explore the use of machine learning models to predict the quality of their wines based on physicochemical tests, which are more accessible and affordable to perform. The goal of the project is to develop a predictive model that can accurately estimate the quality of wine based on dataset. Additionally, the model can be used to identify which physicochemical factors have the greatest impact on wine quality, which can inform decisions about which aspects of the production process to prioritize for improvement.
