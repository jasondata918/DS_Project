IBM Employee Attrition

Kaggle Competition : IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition & Performance

Project Overview

The dataset is a fictional dataset created by IBM data scientists, which contains information on employee demographics, job roles, performance, and other factors that may influence employee attrition. The objective of this dataset is to uncover the factors that contribute to employee attrition and to explore important questions such as the breakdown of distance from home by job role and attrition, or the comparison of average monthly income by education and attrition. By analyzing this dataset, companies can better understand the reasons for employee turnover and potentially take steps to reduce it, such as improving employee satisfaction, work-life balance, or compensation.
