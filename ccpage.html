Predicting Student Academic Success

How can we predict students' academic success and identify the factors that contribute to their success?

Project Overview

In this project aims to use machine learning algorithms to predict students' academic success. Dataset is various socio-economic factors such as parental education, income, and occupation, as well as students' demographic information such as gender, age and nationality. To achieve this goal, I will use various machine learning algorithms such as logistic regression, decision trees, and random forests, and evaluate their performance using metrics such as accuracy, precision, and recall. I will also perform feature selection to identify the most important factors that contribute to student success. By doing so, I hope to provide insights that can help educators and policymakers make informed decisions about how to support students from diverse backgrounds and ensure they have equal opportunities to succeed.
